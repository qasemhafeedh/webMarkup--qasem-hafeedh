Welcome to our website

  Moving to a new city is both exciting and a bit tiring. Whether you're here for a job, school, or just a fresh start, this site is here to help you settle in and feel at home. We’ve gathered all the important tips and information you’ll need to make your transition as smooth as possible.

You’ll find answers to all the common questions that come up when moving to a new place. Whether you're looking for house furnitures, getting around, or just curious about the local spots to hang out, we’ve got it covered.

Here’s a quick look at what you can explore:

  1. House furnitures: Tips on where to live and the best places to buy your furniture.
  2. Local historical places.
  3. Things to Do: Discover the best cafes, parks, and entertainment spots in the city.
  4. If you are coming from overseas and are interested in learning Dutch, we provide tips about schools offering Dutch courses.

  Moving to a new city can be overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. Start with our City Guide or take a look at our Getting Started section to find all the basics you’ll need for your first few weeks.

Having lived here for 3 years, I know it takes time to adjust, but soon you’ll find your own rhythm and start enjoying everything that Haarlem offers. I hope this guide makes things easier and helps you feel at home a little sooner!
